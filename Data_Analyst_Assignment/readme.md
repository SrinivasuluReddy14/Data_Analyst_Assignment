## 🧾 SQL Tasks

### 🏨 Hotel Management System

* Designed relational schema for users, bookings, items, and billing
* Queries implemented:

  * Last booked room per user
  * Total billing per booking (monthly)
  * Bills above threshold using HAVING
  * Most and least ordered items per month
  * Second highest bill per month

---

### 🏥 Clinic Management System

* Designed schema for clinics, customers, sales, and expenses
* Queries implemented:

  * Revenue per sales channel
  * Top 10 customers by spending
  * Monthly revenue, expense, profit, and status
  * Most profitable clinic per city
  * Second least profitable clinic per state

---

## 📊 Spreadsheet Tasks

* Used **INDEX-MATCH** to map ticket data into feedbacks
* Performed time-based analysis:

  * Identified tickets created and closed on same day
  * Identified tickets created and closed in same hour
* Used **COUNTIF** for aggregation
